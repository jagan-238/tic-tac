Tic Tac Toe

Game Rules

Learn how to play Tic Tac Toe before starting the game!

Objective

Align 3 of your symbols (X) horizontally, vertically, or diagonally before the computer (O) does.

Rules

Player is X, Computer is O.
Player moves first.
Click on an empty cell to place your X.
Computer will automatically make its move after you.
If all cells are filled without a winner, the game is a draw.
Score is tracked for wins, losses, and draws.
Try to block the computer from forming 3 in a row.
Start in the center or corners for a better chance to win.
Have fun and try different strategies!
